How to Play

1. Sign Fighters

Pick a contract offer from a promotion, then sign a fighter. You can build up to 10 active fighters in your gym.

2. Train Your Roster

Use Training to sharpen up to 10 fighters each week. Booked fighters can train through a 4-session fight camp before their bout.

Training Limit

Your gym can train all 10 active roster fighters per week. Use Train All Available when you want the whole gym working at once.

3. Take Fight Offers

Open Fight Offers when a fighter is ready. Offers try to match opponents near your fighter's rating, then begin a longer 4-week camp.

Longer Fights

Fight rounds now depend on fight size. Lower-card fights can be 4, 6, or 8 rounds, medium fights can be 8 or 10, feature fights can be 10 or 12, and title fights are always 12 rounds.

4. Sim Weeks

Simulate Next Week to run scheduled promotion shows, resolve booked fights, update rankings, and advance contracts.

5. Watch Cards

Use Cards to check record, earnings, title rank, market value, contract status, condition, next fight, and camp progress.

6. Manage the World

Gyms, free agents, promotions, retirements, new prospects, champions, rankings, news, and show schedules keep changing as weeks pass.

Tip: Save from the main menu or in-game top bar before closing the browser.
